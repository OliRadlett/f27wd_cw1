Bombardier Ski-Doo MX Z(E) 600

This snowmobile is featured in wintery Icelandic action scenes of the movie Die Another Day. It can be seen in the museum; Bombardier made a specially trimmed “007 Special Edition” variant of this sled. The Bombardier designer kept agent James Bond in mind when creating the look of this special edition model – a black and silver body accented with red and chrome.
