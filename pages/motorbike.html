BMW MC 1200 Cruiser Motorcycle:

The BMW motorcycle was stolen by James Bond and was used in the chase sequence through the streets of Saigon, Vietnam. It was ridden by James Bond and Wai Lin, a Chinese secret agent.

You can find this Motorcycle at the museum while visiting. This same motorcycle Bond jumps on from the rooftop! During the movie, Tomorrow Never Dies.

The motorbike has the following specifications:

  • 2-cylinder, 1170 cc engine
  • 5 speed gearboxes
  • Top speed is 173 km/h
  • 17 litre fuel capacity
